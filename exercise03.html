Last time I wrote a poem. But now I need some more text, so I'm going to write some stuff here.

The poem I wrote is called a Limerick.

A limerick is a five-line poem that consists of a single stanza, an AABBA rhyme scheme, and whose subject is a short, pithy tale or description. - Poetry 101 by Masterclass.

Limericks are usually known by the There once was a... opening and they are supposed to be quite witty.

Here is my Limerick:

There once was an HTML

Whose job was to know how to tell

The way to display

Everything that I say

As long as I bracket it well
